Objectives

01Entrepreneurship as a Concept

- Help students unlock their entrepreneurial DNA and develop an entrepreneurial mindset
- Equip students with the skills and thought-processes required to take an idea from inception to a working prototype

02Thoughts to Reality

- Help students develop a solution-focused and action-oriented mindset
- Make students adaptive to changes in technology
- Develop a willingness to exploit opportunities in a technology enabled world

03People Skills

- Teach students to make a comprehensive business plan and communicate their ideas effectively
- Develop an attitude that is conducive to teamwork and collaboration
